Bosch Business Portal

A free area for Consultants, Planners & Specifiers, designed to make information gathering for newbuild & retrofit projects; fast, accurate and in full.

Portal features at a glance


Create New Project

Create a bespoke project file.

View My Projects

A perminent project specific folder, where you/ your team can access all CAD/ BIM files, drawings, technical data sheets, product specifications etc for a unique job.

Browse Product Library

Self service area to search for and download specific product technical files.

Request Project Support

Select your project and select a support request. Our team will gather the information, save it in your project fold on your behalf and send an email confirmation when the request is complete.

Request CPD

Browse and book a selection of certification courses on the latest industry developments for yourself/ your team.

Individual/ team collaborative

allow multiple team access/ edit/ contribute to the project folders.
